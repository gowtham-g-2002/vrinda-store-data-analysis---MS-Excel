📊 Excel Sales Dashboard Project | Vrinda Store Annual Report 2022

This project features an interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze Vrinda Store's 2022 annual sales performance. The goal was to clean raw sales data, derive meaningful insights through pivot tables and charts, and present them in an interactive dashboard with slicers — helping the business understand its customers and grow sales in 2023.

🎯 Project Objective
Create a data-driven annual report that helps Vrinda Store identify key customer segments, top-performing states, and highest-contributing sales channels to make informed business decisions for 2023.

📈 Key Metrics at a Glance
• ~65% of purchases made by women
• ~50% of orders from the 30–49 age group (Adults)
• ~35% of sales from top 3 states: Maharashtra, Karnataka & Uttar Pradesh
• ~80% of orders via Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra

🔍 Key Features
• Orders vs Sales: Single combo chart comparing monthly sales amount and order count
• Sales: Men vs Women: Pie chart showing gender-wise purchase split
• Order Status: Pie chart breaking down delivered, cancelled, refunded & returned orders
• Top 5 States: Horizontal bar chart showing highest revenue-generating states
• Orders: Age vs Gender: Clustered bar chart analyzing orders across Adult, Senior & Teenager segments
• Orders: Channels: Donut chart showing sales split across Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho, Nalli, Ajio & others
• Dynamic Filters: Interactive slicers for Month, Channel, and Category

💡 Sample Questions Answered
• Which month got the highest sales and orders?
• Who purchased more — men or women in 2022?
• What are the different order statuses in 2022?
• Which are the top 10 states contributing to sales?
• What is the relation between age and gender based on number of orders?
• Which channel contributes to maximum sales?
• What is the highest selling category?

🎯 Business Recommendation
Target women aged 30–49 living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh by showing ads, offers, and coupons available on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra to maximize sales growth in 2023.
